# Sample Product Data for RushX E-Commerce Platform

## How to Add Products

1. Go to: `http://127.0.0.1:8000/admin/`
2. Login with your superuser credentials
3. Click **"Products"** → **"Add Product +"**
4. Fill in the details from the data below
5. Click **"Save"**
